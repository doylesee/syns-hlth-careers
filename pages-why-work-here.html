Discover what our 22,000+ employees already know: NewCo is an inspiring place to be. We work hard, and smart, all in the name of getting much-needed therapies to those who need them most. A career with NewCo means your everyday work improves patients’ lives around the world. Selecting us as an employer secures a career in which you’re guaranteed to:

  • Collaborate with passionate problem solvers.

    Partner with the most diverse team of experts in the industry. From the top down, our people are inspiring. Everyone has a voice and is encouraged to use it.

    • We are 22,000 employees strong including more than 750 MDs and PhDs, Pharma Voice 100 Recipients, HBA Rising Stars and Luminaries, and PM 360 Elite Award Winners.
    • Selling Solutions has a network of 50,000 clinicians includes clinical nurse educators, medical science liaisons, and medical directors.
    • We are one of the largest outsourced sales service providers in the biopharmaceutical, dental, medical device and diagnostics, and animal health industries, with an average of 60 sales teams and over 4,000 active field personnel deployed at any one time.
    • We work with the spirit of partnership. We integrate seamlessly with our clients and do what’s best for their success. Our people provide the brilliant ideas, valuable insights and endless energy that shorten the distance from lab to life.
  • Be Empowered And Thrive.

    Here, there’s no shortage of challenging work, learning and opportunities. Evolve in a global company that is always looking for ways to work smarter and more efficiently.

    • NewCo provides an opportunity-rich environment where employees can grow as professionals in one of many disciplines and pursue long-term career goals throughout our worldwide network of locations.
    • We have the ability to support clients in over 110 countries.
    • Our client roster consists of more than 550 global pharmaceutical companies and all top 50 biopharmaceutical companies.
    • NewCo Selling Solutions’ network spans across all 15 major therapeutic areas.
    • NewCo Communications is the largest independent healthcare communications network in the world.
  • Over deliver and Out-Perform.

    Fight the status quo in a highly competitive and ever-changing environment. Be agile and take initiative to set and exceed targets for success.

    • Within the last 5 years, Selling Solutions has successfully launched over 55 new products and/or companies for its client partners into crowded and competitive markets.
    • Consulting has partnered with nearly 400 clients successfully delivering more than 6,800 projects. We’ve supported more than 500 product launches, more than 2,500 clinical trial applications, and more than 250 development plans.
    • We deployed 120+ sales teams in the last 5 years across all major therapeutic areas - more than the top 25 pharmaceutical companies combined.
    • We provide any kind of field force or clinical team that a client may need to promote a product or educate healthcare stakeholders as well as any services needed to support and train that team.
    • NewCo Communications agencies have won more than 1,000 awards in the past 10 years, including multiple agency of the year awards.
  • Central to it all: shortening the distance from lab to life

    Make a difference as part of the only fully integrated Biopharmaceutical Accelerator.

    • In a recent five-year period, NewCo has helped to develop or commercialize:
      • 82% of Novel New Drugs approved by the FDA; 70% Products Granted Marketing Authorization approved by the EMA
      • 79% Novel New Oncology Drugs approved by the FDA; 84% Oncology Drugs approved by the EMA
      • 88% of Novel New Central Nervous System Drugs approved by the FDA; 70% Central Nervous System Drugs approved by the EMA
      • 100% Novel new Cardiovascular Drugs approved by the FDA; 71% Cardiovascular Drugs approved by the EMA
      • 91% Novel New Respiratory Drugs approved by the FDA
      • 84% Endocrinology Drugs approved by the EMA
